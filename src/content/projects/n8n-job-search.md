This advanced automation template transforms the job hunting process from a manual grind into an intelligent, AI-driven pipeline. By integrating the **JSearch API** with the reasoning power of **Google Gemini**, the workflow autonomously discovers, filters, and evaluates job listings based on your specific professional background.

### Key Features:
- **Intelligent Discovery**: Formulates precise search queries by analyzing your current profile and fetches live listings via RapidAPI.
- **AI Relevance Scoring**: Uses Google Gemini to act as a technical recruiter, providing a detailed match score (1-100) and fit summary for every role.
- **Notion Integration**: Automatically logs high-relevance matches into a structured Notion database for easy tracking and application management.
- **Smart Filtering**: Built-in logic ensures you only see fresh postings (last 14 days) and avoids duplicates by cross-referencing existing Notion entries.
